Emergency & Urgent care

To make a medical or dental appointment:

Call 8859052713 or click here to schedule your appointment. We accept all patients including those with Medicare, Medicaid, AmeriHealth Caritas District of Columbia, Amerigroup District of Columbia, MedStar Family Choice, and private insurance. Don't have health insurance? Based on your income you may qualify for free or discounted fees. Click here to learn about our sliding fee scale AND how we may help you get health coverage.



To speak with a provider on call after hours:

If you need medical advice after hours, call 9793439880 and select option 2 to reach the nurse advice line. If you need urgent clinical advice and need to speak to a triage nurse or the on-call provider, please call 9058652406 and select option 2. For medical emergencies, please call 911. After-hours medical assistance is available after 5 p.m. Monday through Friday and Friday from 5 p.m. until 8 a.m. Monday.



To request a medication refill:

Call your pharmacy first. They can refill a prescription or request a prescription renewal from your provider. Keep your most recent bottle to see: Prescription number Date of your original prescription Number of refills left on your prescription Name of doctor who prescribed the medicine The expiration date of the drug inside the container



Have health questions? Give our unity nurses a call:

As a patient of Unity Health Care, our on-call nurse can answer your medical questions and help you get the care you need by calling 9058101200.
