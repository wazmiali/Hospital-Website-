Compassionate, comprehensive, high-quality health care
Unity Health Care reaches people wherever they are to provide compassionate, comprehensive, high-quality health care that is accessible to all and advances health equity in Washington, DC. As the largest network of community health centers in the District, our team of compassionate and multicultural health professionals place Unity values into action every day to bring whole-person care and wellness to nearly 90,000 patients through 405,000 visits annually through our network of over 20 traditional and non-traditional health sites and a mobile medical outreach vehicle.

OUR SERVICES

Serving the health needs of our communities

At Unity Health Care, we believe every person deserves access to high-quality, comprehensive, and compassionate healthcare services. We provide team-based care through coordinated, patient-centered services in our Patient-Centered Medical Homes, providing an array of health services at one location to meet your needs. Our patient-centered healthcare teams include Pediatricians, Internists, OB/GYN, Certified Nurse Midwives, Nurses, Nurse Practitioners, Family Medicine, Dentists, Dental Hygienists, Oral Surgeons, Pharmacists, Behavioral Health Clinicians, Community Health Workers, Care Coordinators, Case Managers (Referral Specialists) and more.
